# A manuscript is the compiled output produced from ordered leaves.

- A manuscript is generated from the leaves loaded out of `content/`.
- Templates decide how those leaves become chapters, appendices, frontmatter, and backmatter.
- Related commands: CMD-BUILD, CMD-EXPORT.
- Related configuration: CFG-TEMPLATES, CFG-TEMPLATE-COLLECTIONS.
